About

Aiming to encourage a lively discussion around the opportunities and challenges facing women interested in entrepreneurship, we are bringing together five amazing Cypriots who are helping shape the island's entrepreneurial landscape.

From making the transition from the corporate to the entrepreneurial world, moving to Cyprus from abroad, starting their own business, working in tech and heading Cyprus' first incubator, each of our speakers will share their distinct experiences as women working within the island's budding entrepreneurial environment.
